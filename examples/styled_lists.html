Styled lists

Styled unordered and ordered lists that look good in both light and dark modes.

Unordered list

Simple bullet-point list that has no particular order.

  • Unordered item 1
  • Unordered item 2
  • Unordered item 3

Ordered list

List with ordered numbering.

  1. Ordered item 1
  2. Ordered item 2
  3. Ordered item 3

Nesting and text wrapping

Lists can be nested within one another, and if the text wraps to a new line, the padding for the bullet/numbering will still apply to the new line.

  • The weather forecast didn't say that, but the steel plate in his hip did.
    1. He had learned over the years to trust his hip over the weatherman.
    2. It was going to rain, so he better get outside and prepare.
      • Sleeping in his car was never the plan but sometimes things don't work out as planned.
      • This had been his life for the last three months and he was just beginning to get used to it.
  • He didn't actually enjoy it, but he had accepted it and come to terms with it. Or at least he thought he had.
  • All that changed when he put the key into the ignition, turned it and the engine didn't make a sound.
